Описание

A relaxing atmospheric puzzle game with adventure elements. Embark on a mystical journey through Tokyo and beyond to discover the secret Tanabata festival! Explore beautiful locations, solve ancient puzzles, and play magical mini-games where your choices determine the spectacular starry finale.

Особенности

  • ✨ Authentic Japanese atmosphere – from cozy Tokyo streets to mysterious caves and a hidden village under the starry sky
  • 🎮 Three unique levels – featuring different mechanics from social interactions to environmental puzzles
  • 🎵 Soothing soundtrack – traditional Japanese instruments blended with natural ambient sounds
  • 🎆 Dynamic ending – your performance determines the scale of the grand celestial celebration
  • ⏳ Gentle time pressure – the stars won't wait, so reach the festival before the climax!
